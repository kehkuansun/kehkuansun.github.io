Job Market Paper

  • “Lying Aversion and Vague Communication: An Experimental Study” with Guangying Chen
    [abstract] [slides] [video](Draft available upon request.)

We study the interplay between the strategic and the behavioral aspects of potentially vague communication through an online experiment governed by a novel theoretical analysis. The online experiment varies the availability of vague messages and the anonymity of subjects to test the predictions and, in particular, to separate the internal and external costs of dishonesty.
